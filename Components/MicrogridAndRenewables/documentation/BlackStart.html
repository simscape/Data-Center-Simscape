Overview

This block implements black start logic for a grid‑forming Battery Energy Storage System (BESS). When the microgrid is de‑energized (no external voltage reference), the controller creates voltage and frequency references and ramps the terminal voltage in three discrete levels across three scheduled time intervals to safely energize the bus, charge magnetics, and connect downstream loads without excessive inrush.
Key Objectives:
  • Microgrid formation after a complete outage.
  • Controlled energization of transformers and low voltage feeders.
  • Pre‑synchronization preparation before planned resynchronization to the main grid.

Detailed Description

Block Functionality

The controller :
  • Generates voltage magnitude reference |V*| and frequency reference f* for the BESS converter in Voltage and Frequency (VF) mode.
  • Executes a three‑stage voltage ramp with programmable targets and durations.
